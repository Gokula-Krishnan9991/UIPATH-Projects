This Automation is about finding the fastest train and calculating the distance between source and destination.

Steps
1.Bot launches the confirm ticket site
2.It enters the source and destination, based upon the user's Input and date will be five days from the today's date
3.Filtering the duration and extracting the source time and destination time
4.Based upon the source and destination, it calculates the distance and difference between the two distance
5. Finally output the result in the message box
